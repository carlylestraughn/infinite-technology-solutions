About Biocruit Solutions

Gordon Straughn, founder of Biocruit Solutions, brings over 20 years of experience leading executive search in the biotechnology and pharmaceutical industries. He has partnered with organizations from early-stage startups to established leaders like Gilead Sciences to recruit Director through C-suite talent across R&D, Clinical Development, Regulatory Affairs, and corporate functions.

Previously, Gordon built and managed Gilead’s North American R&D Talent Acquisition team of 25—delivering more than 2,700 total FTE hires, including all Clinical Development MDs across five therapeutic areas—and personally recruiting 600+ professionals. He’s recognized for discretion, trusted partnership with executive leadership, and a proven ability to secure transformative talent for high-growth and innovative companies.
