About Biocruit Solutions

Founded in 1996 by Gordon Straughn, Biocruit Solutions partners with small to mid-sized biotech and established biopharma on hard-to-fill leadership roles. Gordon supported Gilead Sciences from 2006–2016—personally recruiting and hiring 608 FTEs and managing a North American R&D talent acquisition team that delivered 2,719 hires during that period. Engagements span Director through SVP levels across R&D and G&A.

How It Works

1) Calibration

30–45 min intake on scope, stakeholders, competencies, compensation, and timeline.

2) Sourcing

Targeted outreach with weekly slate refresh, notes, and market feedback.

3) Interviews

Structured screens, panel alignment, scorecards; candidate experience stewardship.

4) Offer & Close

References, compensation alignment, close plan, and start-date coordination.
